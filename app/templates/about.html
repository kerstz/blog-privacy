About this blog

Hi, welcome to MyBlog.

This is just a personal blog. I made it mostly to write things that are on my mind and sometimes share them with whoever ends up here. Writing helps me clear my head, and it's also a way to talk with people online. I'm a teenager from France, currently studying.

This blog doesn't really have one specific topic. It's mostly about whatever I feel like writing about. Sometimes it might be random thoughts, sometimes things about the internet, privacy, or just things I find interesting.

I'm not an expert in anything and I'm not trying to be one. This blog is simply a place to share ideas and thoughts.

I believe privacy is a fundamental right, and it's something people shouldn't have to give up just to use the internet.

Why Tor for this blog?

First, because I believe privacy is important.

Second, because Tor itself is both disturbing and fascinating at the same time, and I wanted to add my small contribution to this space.

A quick note

Misogyny, racism, or homophobia are not welcome here.

The values that matter most to me are respect and open-mindedness. Everyone has different opinions and perspectives, and that's part of what makes discussions interesting.

There isn't really a big goal behind this blog. It's just a place to write, think, and sometimes share.

If you're here reading this, thanks for stopping by.
